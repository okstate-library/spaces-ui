• A study room reservation can only be booked for up to 4 hours at a time.
  • Only 2 active study room reservations can be made at one time.
  • Study room reservations must allow at least two hours in between the end of a reservation and the beginning of another one.
  • Room keys can be checked out at the Circulation & Information Desk on the first floor of the Library.
  • Study room doors must be locked at the end of each reservation.
  • Smart board equipment is unavailable at this time.
  • If it is your first time checking out a study room, you will be asked to sign a "Statement of Responsibility" when you come to the Circulation & Information Desk. This signed statement will remain active for one calendar year.
  • Reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the beginning of the reservation. At that time, the room may be reassigned.
  • White noise machines are available for checkout with study room reservations.
  • Study rooms are cleaned each night by the Library's custodial team. Cleaning supplies are available by request if you wish to sanitize before or after a reservation.
  • If you do not receive a confirmation email within 30 minutes of making your reservation, please call the Circulation/Information Desk at 405-744-9741.
