• A study room reservation can be booked for up to 2 hours at a time.
  • Study room keys can be checked out at the Circulation/Information Desk on the first floor of the Vet Med Library.
  • Reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the beginning of the reservation. At that time, the room may be reassigned.
  • If it is your first time checking out a study room, you will be asked to sign a "Statement of Responsibility" at the Circulation/Information desk. This signed statement will remains active for one calendar year.
  • White noise machines are available for checkout with study room reservations.
  • Each study room is equipped with a wall monitor. Please turn the monitor off after use.
  • Study room doors must be locked at the end of each reservation. Study room keys must be returned on time to the Circulation/Information Desk.
  • If you have any questions regarding study room reservations and/or policies, please visit the Vet Med Library Circulation/Information Desk or call 405-744-6655.
