• Studio rooms may be booked for up to 4 hours.
  • Only two studio room reservations may be active at a time.
  • Room keys are checked out at the Creative Studios desk on the first floor of the library, located in the southeast corner.
  • Studio doors must be locked at the end of each reservation.
  • If it is your first time checking out a studio room, you will be asked to sign a Statement of Responsibility when you check out the key. The signed statement remains active for one calendar year.
  • Reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the scheduled start time, after which the room may be reassigned.
  • Do not adjust, unplug, or relocate any equipment or devices in the studio rooms; doing so will result in loss of privileges.
  • If you do not receive a confirmation email within 30 minutes of making your reservation, please call the Creative Studios desk at 405-744-7615.
