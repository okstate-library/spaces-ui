• A Reading Room reservation can be booked for up to 3 hours at a time. If more time is needed please make the request directly to the librarian via email sara.mautino@okstate.edu
  • Reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the beginning of the reservation. At that time, the room may be reassigned.
  • If it is your first time checking out the Reading Room, you will be asked to sign a “Statement of Responsibility” at the circulation desk.
  • The room must be left clean after each use. Trash disposed of, whiteboard erased, and chairs pushed in.
  • If you have any question regarding Reading Room reservations and/or policies, please visit the Architecture Library circulation desk or call 405-744-6074.
